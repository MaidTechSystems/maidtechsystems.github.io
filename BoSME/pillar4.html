4. SUSTAINABLE ENVIRONMENT


About Pillar Goals Progress
We shall promote and actively pursue a sustainable environment with conservation and preservation at the heart of every action in our pursuit of inclusive social and economic development for us and the future generations. 1. Promote and champion responsible interaction with the environment to avoid the depletion or degradation of natural resources.
2. Enhance the quality of living by having a clean and healthy environment.
3. Conserve the environment and reverse the impact of climate change for the benefit of present and future generations.

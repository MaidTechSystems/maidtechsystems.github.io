6. RECONSTRUCT, REHABILITATE & RESETTLE


About Pillar Goals Progress
We shall work to ensure faster reconstruction, rehabilitation and resettlement of displaced individuals and families in secured and affordable
and self- sustaining communities; such that we achieve voluntary resettlement of at least 50% of IDPs by 2022 and no IDP camps by 2026.		 1. Create a peaceful, secured, inclusive and economically self-reliant communities.
2. Ensure early and sustainable return, resettlement and re- integration of Orphans, IDP’s & returning refugees.
3. Successfully, rehabilitated returnees as positive contributor in the communities and active participants in the State’s economy.
